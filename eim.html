Electrical Installation and Maintenance Course

Enroll in our Electrical Installation and Maintenance Course to gain essential skills in wiring, circuitry, and electrical system repair. This course is designed to prepare you for a career as an electrician or electrical technician.

Key components of this course include:

  • Basic and advanced electrical principles
  • Installation and maintenance of electrical systems
  • Reading and interpreting electrical diagrams
  • Safety procedures and regulations
  • Troubleshooting and repair techniques

Through a combination of theoretical knowledge and hands-on training, you'll develop the skills necessary to work in various industries, including construction, manufacturing, and maintenance.
