Diploma in Radiology & Technology

About Diploma in Radiology Technology:

The Diploma in Radiology Technology program is a comprehensive course designed to train individuals in various aspects of radiation science and technology. This program equips students with the necessary skills and knowledge to work effectively in radiation therapy centers, diagnostic imaging facilities, and research laboratories.

Course Curriculum:

  • Radiation Physics
  • Radiation Biology
  • Diagnostic Radiology Techniques
  • Therapeutic Radiology Techniques
  • Radiation Safety and Protection
  • Medical Imaging Technology
  • Radiation Therapy Planning
  • Quality Control in Radiation Technology

Course Highlights:

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Eligibility: Completion of 10th grade or equivalent from a recognized board
  • Curriculum: Comprehensive training in radiation science, theoretical knowledge, and practical experience
  • Certification: Upon successful completion, students receive a Diploma in Radiation Technology

Admission Process:

  1. Eligible candidates can apply directly after completing their 10th grade examinations.
  2. Admission may be based on merit or entrance examination conducted by the institution.
  3. Shortlisted candidates may be called for a counseling session or interview.

Career Opportunities:

Graduates of the Diploma in Radiation Technology program have promising career prospects in various healthcare settings, including:

  1. Radiation Therapy Centers
  2. Diagnostic Imaging Facilities
  3. Research Laboratories
  4. Hospitals
  5. Clinics
  6. Government Health Departments
