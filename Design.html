Introduction to B.Des

B.Des, or Bachelor of Design, is a 4-year undergraduate degree program that provides a comprehensive understanding of various aspects of design, creativity, and aesthetics. This program prepares students for a variety of career opportunities in the design industry, including fashion, product, graphic, and interior design.

By choosing a B.Des degree, you'll gain a thorough understanding of your chosen specialization, develop essential design skills, and position yourself for a rewarding career in the field of design.

Entrance Exams for B.Des

To secure admission to top design colleges in India, students may need to clear one or more of the following entrance exams:

  • NID DAT (National Institute of Design - Design Aptitude Test)
  • UCEED (Undergraduate Common Entrance Examination for Design)
  • NIFT Entrance Exam (National Institute of Fashion Technology)
  • CEPT Entrance Exam (Centre for Environmental Planning and Technology)

Explore the exam patterns, eligibility criteria, and preparation tips to ace these competitive tests.
