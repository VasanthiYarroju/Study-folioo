FAQ's asked :-

1.What are the different streams available after 10th/12th?
- After 10th, students can choose from science, commerce, and arts streams. After 12th, they can further specialize in various fields within these streams or opt for vocational courses.

2. Which stream should I choose after 10th/12th?
- The choice of stream depends on your interests, strengths, and career aspirations. Consider your academic performance, subjects you enjoy, and future goals when making this decision.

3.What are the career prospects for each stream after 10th/12th?
- Career prospects vary depending on the stream and specialization chosen. Science stream can lead to careers in engineering, medicine, or research, while commerce stream prepares students for finance, commerce, or management roles. Arts stream can lead to careers in journalism, social work, or civil services.

4. What are vocational courses, and how do they differ from regular courses?
- Vocational courses focus on providing practical skills and training for specific industries or job roles. They often have a shorter duration compared to traditional academic courses and are designed to prepare students for immediate employment.

5. What are the entrance exams required for admission to courses after 12th?
- Entrance exams vary depending on the course and institution. Common entrance exams include JEE (for engineering), NEET (for medical), CLAT (for law), and various state-level entrance exams for other professional courses.
