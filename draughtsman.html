ITI Courses - Draughtsman (Civil/Mechanical)

Description:

Draughtsman (Civil/Mechanical) course at ITI prepares students with essential skills and knowledge required in the field of civil and mechanical drafting. The course curriculum includes drafting techniques, CAD software training, engineering drawings, blueprint reading, structural detailing, and project planning. Students are trained to create accurate technical drawings and plans used in various industries such as construction, manufacturing, and engineering.

Duration:

The duration of the Draughtsman (Civil/Mechanical) course typically ranges from 1 to 2 years, depending on the specific institute and program structure.

Eligibility Criteria:

To enroll in this course, candidates usually need to have completed their 10th standard (or equivalent) education from a recognized board. Some institutes may also require candidates to have a basic understanding of mathematics and science.

Career Opportunities:

Graduates of this course can pursue careers as draughtsmen in various industries including architecture, engineering consulting firms, construction companies, manufacturing firms, and more. They can work on projects ranging from building design to mechanical part drafting. With experience and expertise, they can advance to positions such as senior draughtsman, CAD technician, design engineer, or project manager.
