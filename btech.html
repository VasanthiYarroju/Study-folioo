Introduction to B.Tech

B.Tech, or Bachelor of Technology, is a 4-year undergraduate degree program that provides a strong foundation in engineering principles and practical skills. This program prepares students for diverse career opportunities in the technology and engineering industries.

By choosing a B.Tech degree, you'll gain a comprehensive understanding of your chosen specialization, develop essential technical skills, and position yourself for a rewarding career in the field of your interest.

Entrance Exams for B.Tech

To secure admission to top engineering colleges in India, students need to clear one or more of the following entrance exams:

  • JEE Main

  • BITSAT
  • State-level Engineering Entrance Exams
