Automotive Technology Course

Our Automotive Technology Course provides comprehensive training in automotive repair, maintenance, and diagnostics. Whether you're passionate about cars or looking to start a career in the automotive industry, this course is designed to equip you with the necessary skills and knowledge.

Key topics covered in this course include:

  • Engine repair and maintenance
  • Electrical systems and diagnostics
  • Brake and suspension systems
  • Transmission repair
  • Computerized vehicle control systems

Through a combination of classroom instruction and hands-on training, you'll learn how to diagnose and repair various automotive issues, ensuring that you're prepared for a successful career in the automotive industry.
