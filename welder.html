ITI Course: Welder

The Welder course at ITI provides comprehensive training in various welding techniques and processes used in industries.

Course Curriculum

  • Introduction to Welding
  • Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW)
  • Gas Metal Arc Welding (GMAW)
  • Gas Tungsten Arc Welding (GTAW)
  • Flux-Cored Arc Welding (FCAW)
  • Oxyfuel Gas Welding and Cutting
  • Welding Inspection and Quality Control
  • Practical Welding Projects

Join us to gain the skills needed for a career as a welder!
