About CEC Stream

The CEC stream in intermediate education focuses on Commerce, Economics, and Civics subjects. It provides students with a strong foundation in commerce, economics, and political science and prepares them for various competitive exams and careers in business, economics, public administration, and related fields.

Subjects in CEC Stream

  • Commerce
  • Economics
  • Civics

Scope and Career Opportunities

Students who opt for the CEC stream have a wide range of career options available to them, including:

  • Commerce
  • Economics
  • Business Administration
  • Accountancy
  • Public Administration
  • Finance
  • And many more...

Benefits of Choosing CEC Stream

  • Understanding of economic principles and business concepts
  • Prepares students for competitive exams like CA, CS, and CMA
  • Opens up opportunities for pursuing careers in finance, accounting, and management
  • Provides a solid foundation for understanding economic policies and governance
