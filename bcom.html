Introduction to B.Com

B.Com, or Bachelor of Commerce, is a 3-year undergraduate degree program that provides a comprehensive understanding of various aspects of commerce, business, and economics. This program prepares students for a variety of career opportunities in the finance, accounting, and business sectors.

By choosing a B.Com degree, you'll gain a thorough understanding of your chosen specialization, develop essential business skills, and position yourself for a rewarding career in the field of commerce.

Entrance Exams for B.Com

To secure admission to top commerce colleges in India, students may need to clear one or more of the following entrance exams:

  • CUET (Common University Entrance Test)
  • IPU CET (Indraprastha University Common Entrance Test)
  • DU JAT (Delhi University Joint Admission Test)
  • NPAT (NMIMS Programs After Twelfth)

Explore the exam patterns, eligibility criteria, and preparation tips to ace these competitive tests.
