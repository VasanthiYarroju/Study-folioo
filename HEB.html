About HEB Stream

The HEB stream in intermediate education focuses on History, Economics, and Business subjects. It provides students with a strong foundation in history, economics, and business and prepares them for various competitive exams and careers in history, economics, business administration, entrepreneurship, and related fields.

Subjects in HEB Stream

  • History
  • Economics
  • Business

The History, Economics, and Business (HEB) course at St. Joseph's Evening College is designed for students who want to prepare for competitive exams like the UPSC tests. The History department aims to increase students' awareness of the world's and country's development and growth. Scope and Career Opportunities

Students who opt for the HEB stream have a wide range of career options available to them, including:

  • History
  • Economics
  • Business Administration
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Marketing
  • Finance
  • And many more...

Benefits of Choosing HEB Stream

  • Understanding of historical events, economic theories, and business principles
  • Prepares students for competitive exams like UPSC, PSC, and CLAT
  • Opens up opportunities for pursuing careers in history, economics, business administration, and entrepreneurship
  • Provides a solid foundation for understanding governance, economic policies, and business management
