ITI Course: Mechanic (Motor Vehicle)

The Mechanic (Motor Vehicle) course at ITI provides comprehensive training in the repair and maintenance of various types of vehicles.

Course Curriculum

  • Vehicle Engine Systems
  • Chassis and Suspension Systems
  • Transmission Systems
  • Electrical Systems
  • Fuel Systems
  • Vehicle Diagnostics and Repair
  • Preventive Maintenance
  • Internship Opportunities

Join us to gain the skills needed for a career as a mechanic!
