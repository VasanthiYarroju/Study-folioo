About Diploma in Pharmacy:

The Diploma in Pharmacy program is a comprehensive course designed to train individuals in various aspects of pharmaceutical science and practice. This program equips students with the necessary skills and knowledge to work effectively in pharmacy settings such as retail pharmacies, hospitals, and pharmaceutical companies.

Course Curriculum:

  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry
  • Pharmacology
  • Pharmaceutical Analysis
  • Pharmacognosy
  • Pharmacy Practice
  • Pharmaceutical Microbiology
  • Hospital and Clinical Pharmacy

Course Highlights:

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Eligibility: Completion of 10th grade or equivalent from a recognized board
  • Curriculum: Comprehensive training in pharmaceutical science, theoretical knowledge, and practical experience
  • Certification: Upon successful completion, students receive a Diploma in Pharmacy

Admission Process:

  1. Eligible candidates can apply directly after completing their 10th grade examinations.
  2. Admission may be based on merit or entrance examination conducted by the institution.
  3. Shortlisted candidates may be called for a counseling session or interview.

Career Opportunities:

Graduates of the Diploma in Pharmacy program have promising career prospects in various pharmacy settings, including:

  1. Retail Pharmacies
  2. Hospitals
  3. Pharmaceutical Companies
  4. Research Laboratories
  5. Government Health Departments
