ITI Course: Fitter

The Fitter course at ITI provides comprehensive training in fitting and assembling components in machinery and equipment.

Course Curriculum

  • Introduction to Fitting
  • Measurement Techniques
  • Use of Hand Tools and Machinery
  • Reading and Interpreting Engineering Drawings
  • Assembly of Mechanical Components
  • Quality Control and Inspection
  • Maintenance of Equipment
  • Practical Workshop Training

Join us to gain the skills needed for a career in fitting and assembly!
