About HEC Stream

The HEC stream in intermediate education focuses on History, Economics, and Commerce subjects. It provides students with a strong foundation in history, economics, and commerce and prepares them for various competitive exams and careers in history, political science, public administration, law, and related fields.

Subjects in HEC Stream

  • History
  • Economics
  • Commerce

Scope and Career Opportunities

Students who opt for the HEC stream have a wide range of career options available to them, including:

  • History
  • Political Science
  • Public Administration
  • Law
  • International Relations
  • Business Administration
  • And many more...

Benefits of Choosing HEC Stream

  • Understanding of historical events, economic theories, and business concepts
  • Prepares students for competitive exams like UPSC, PSC, and CLAT
  • Opens up opportunities for pursuing careers in history, politics, law, and administration
  • Provides a solid foundation for understanding societal dynamics and governance
