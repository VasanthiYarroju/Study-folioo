About DMLT:

The Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT) is a comprehensive program designed to train individuals in various aspects of laboratory medicine and technology. The course provides students with the necessary skills and knowledge to work effectively in clinical laboratories, research institutes, and healthcare settings.

Course Curriculum:

  • Introduction to Medical Laboratory Science
  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • Biochemistry
  • Microbiology
  • Hematology
  • Clinical Pathology
  • Immunology
  • Histopathology
  • Laboratory Management and Quality Control

Course Highlights:

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Eligibility: Completion of 10th grade or equivalent from a recognized board
  • Curriculum: Comprehensive training in medical laboratory techniques, theoretical knowledge, and practical skills
  • Certification: Upon successful completion, students receive a Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology

Admission Process:

  1. Eligible candidates can apply directly after completing their 10th grade examinations.
  2. Admission may be based on merit or entrance examination conducted by the institution.
  3. Shortlisted candidates may be called for a counseling session or interview.

Career Opportunities:

Graduates of DMLT have promising career prospects in various healthcare settings, including:

  1. Hospitals
  2. Diagnostic Laboratories
  3. Blood Banks
  4. Research Institutes
  5. Pharmaceutical Companies
  6. Public Health Departments
