About HEP Stream

The HEP stream in intermediate education focuses on History, Economics, and Political Science subjects. It provides students with a strong foundation in history, economics, and political science and prepares them for various competitive exams and careers in history, politics, public administration, law, and related fields.

Subjects in HEP Stream

  • History
  • Economics
  • Political Science

The History, Economics, and Political Science (HEP) course at St. Joseph's Evening College is designed for students who want to prepare for competitive exams like the UPSC tests. The History department aims to increase students' awareness of the world's and country's development and growth. Psychology is a social science that involves understanding how and why people behave in social settings and groups. Scope and Career Opportunities

Students who opt for the HEP stream have a wide range of career options available to them, including:

  • History
  • Political Science
  • Public Administration
  • Law
  • International Relations
  • Business Administration
  • And many more...

Benefits of Choosing HEP Stream

  • Understanding of historical events, economic theories, and political dynamics
  • Prepares students for competitive exams like UPSC, PSC, and CLAT
  • Opens up opportunities for pursuing careers in history, politics, law, and administration
  • Provides a solid foundation for understanding governance, public policy, and international relations
