ITI Course: Machinist

The Machinist course at ITI provides comprehensive training in operating and maintaining machine tools such as lathes, milling machines, and grinders.

Course Curriculum

  • Machine Shop Safety
  • Blueprint Reading and Interpretation
  • Lathe Operations
  • Milling Machine Operations
  • Grinding Operations
  • Drilling and Boring Operations
  • CNC Machining Basics
  • Quality Control and Inspection

Join us to gain the skills needed for a career as a machinist!
